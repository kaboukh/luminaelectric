RGIE Compliance

The RGIE (General Regulation on Electrical Installations) is the Belgian standard defining safety rules for electrical installations. An RGIE inspection is mandatory in the following cases:

  • Sale or rental of a property
  • New installation or major renovation (installation over 25 years old)
  • Significant modification to the existing installation
  • Periodic inspection every 25 years for a residential property

The cost varies depending on the size and type of installation, generally between €150 and €350 for a standard home. If non-conformities are found, remediation works typically cost between €500 and €3,000 depending on the scope of changes needed.

Absolutely. After every intervention we hand you all the required regulatory documents:

  • Single-line diagram of the installation
  • Circuit position diagram
  • RGIE certificate of conformity
  • Official inspection report
  • Invoices and material warranties
