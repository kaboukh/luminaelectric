Electrical panel replacement in Belgium: price & complete guide 2026

An outdated electrical panel is the leading cause of RGIE non-conformities. Here's what a proper replacement costs — and what it must include.

Home › Blog › Electrical panel replacement

Why replace your electrical panel?

The electrical panel is the heart of your installation. Replacement becomes necessary when:

  • The panel dates from before 1981 (wire fuses or ceramic fuses)
  • Circuit breakers trip regularly without a clear reason
  • No 30mA RCD (residual current device) is present
  • The panel is full — no room to add new circuits
  • You are installing an EV charging point, heat pump or solar panels

Recommended brands in Belgium

In Belgium, the three leading brands for enclosures and switchgear are Hager, Schneider Electric and Legrand. For switches and sockets, the Belgian brand Niko is a benchmark known for its quality and longevity.

What a proper quote must include

  • ✅ Supply and installation of the new enclosure (with ~20% spare capacity)
  • ✅ All necessary circuit breakers and 30mA RCDs
  • ✅ Distribution busbar (three-phase if applicable)
  • ✅ Earth bonding verified
  • ✅ Updated single-line diagram
  • ✅ Testing of every circuit
  • ❌ Not normally included: wiring of existing circuits, conduit replacement
