Our story

Raising the standards of electricity in Belgium.

Lumina Electric was founded with a clear ambition: to raise quality standards in electrical and renovation projects across Belgium.

Every intervention is treated as a strategic investment. An electrical installation must not only work; it must be impeccable, RGIE/AREI-compliant, scalable and value-adding for your property.

Beyond electricity, Lumina Electric also coordinates full turnkey renovations through a network of carefully selected partner trades.

  • In-depth technical analysis before every project
  • Durable, structured solutions
  • Compliance without compromise
  • Execution mastered down to the last detail

Our core values

Every project is guided by three essential pillars.

Quality

First-class materials, careful workmanship and an installation built to last. We make no compromises on execution quality.
