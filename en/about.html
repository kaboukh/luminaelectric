About Lumina Electric

Your trusted partner for all your electrical work in Belgium

Our Story

Lumina Electric was founded with a clear ambition: to raise quality standards in electrical projects and renovations in Belgium.

Each intervention is designed as a strategic investment. An electrical installation must not only function; it must be impeccable, compliant with RGIE / AREI, scalable and valuable for your property.

We adopt a comprehensive and demanding approach:

  • In-depth technical analysis
  • Sustainable and structured solutions
  • Uncompromising compliance
  • Mastered execution down to the smallest details

Beyond electricity, Lumina Electric also manages turnkey general renovations through a network of partners selected for their excellence.

One single contact. Rigorous coordination. A coherent vision.

Our commitment is clear: transform your work into real added value for your property, while providing you with total peace of mind.
