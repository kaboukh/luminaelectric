Frequently asked questions

Have questions?

Answers to the most common questions from our clients.

RGIE (Belgian electrical safety regulation) sets the safety standards for all electrical installations. An inspection is mandatory when: selling or renting a property, new installation or major renovation, significant changes to the installation, and every 25 years for homes.

The cost varies by property size and installation type, typically between €150 and €350 for a standard home. If non-compliance is found, remediation works generally range from €500 to €3,000.

We respond within 24 hours of your request. For larger projects we schedule a site visit within 2-3 days and deliver a detailed quote within 48h of the visit.

Yes, we offer a 24/7 emergency service. For serious electrical faults we respond within 2 to 4 hours, including weekends and public holidays. An emergency call-out surcharge may apply outside regular hours.

Absolutely. After every job we provide all regulatory documents: single-line diagram, position diagram, compliance certificate, inspection report and guarantees.
