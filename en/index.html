Frequently Asked Questions

Do You Have Questions?

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions

RGIE (General Regulation on Electrical Installations) is the Belgian standard that defines safety rules for electrical installations. A RGIE inspection is mandatory during:

  • Sale or rental of a property
  • New installation or major renovation (> 25 years)
  • Significant modification of the installation
  • Periodic inspection every 25 years (residential)

The cost of an RGIE inspection varies according to the surface area and type of installation, generally between €150 and €350 for a standard home. If non-compliances are detected, expect between €500 and €3000 depending on the work required for compliance.
